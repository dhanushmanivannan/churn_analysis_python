# Customer Churn Analysis — Microsoft Cashback Lever

End-to-end churn analysis on 1,000,000 customer records to identify 
why customers leave and what the business can do about it.

## Tools
Python · Pandas · Matplotlib · Jupyter Notebook

## Key Findings
- Month-to-Month contracts churn at 38% vs 7% for Two Year
- Churn risk drops from 24.1% to 6.7% after month 15
- 63,913 early churners = ~$4.8M monthly revenue at risk
